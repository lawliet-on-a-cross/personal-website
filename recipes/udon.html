Udon

Description

Udon is a thick Japanese noodle soup made with wheat flour noodles. The noodles are served in a hot dashi-based broth and topped with various ingredients. The soup is known for its simple yet comforting flavors and chewy noodle texture.

Ingredients

  • Udon noodles
  • Dashi stock
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Green onions
  • Kamaboko (fish cake)
  • Tempura flakes
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Kombu (kelp)
  • Bonito flakes
  • Japanese seven spice (shichimi)

Steps

  • Step 1: Prepare dashi broth
  • Step 2: Cook udon noodles
  • Step 3: Season the broth
  • Step 4: Combine and garnish
