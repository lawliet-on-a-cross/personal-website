Bun Bo Hue

Description

Bun Bo Hue is a spicy Vietnamese soup that is made with pork and beef broth. It is flavored with lemongrass, shrimp paste, and chili oil. The soup is served with rice noodles, beef shank, and pork hock. It is garnished with cilantro, green onions, and lime wedges.

Ingredients

  • Beef shank
  • Pork hock
  • Lemongrass
  • Shrimp paste
  • Chili oil
  • Rice noodles
  • Cilantro
  • Green onions
  • Lime wedges
  • Fish sauce
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sugar
