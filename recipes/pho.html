Pho

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese soup consisting of bone broth, rice noodles, and thinly sliced beef or chicken. The broth is flavored with various spices including star anise, cinnamon, and ginger. It's typically served with fresh herbs, bean sprouts, and lime wedges.

Ingredients

  • Beef bones (marrow and knuckle)
  • Rice noodles
  • Thinly sliced beef (sirloin or brisket)
  • Star anise
  • Cinnamon sticks
  • Ginger
  • Onion
  • Fish sauce
  • Bean sprouts
  • Thai basil
  • Cilantro
  • Green onions
  • Lime wedges
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Sriracha sauce

Steps

  • Step 1: Prepare the bone broth (6-8 hours)
  • Step 2: Toast and add spices
  • Step 3: Cook the rice noodles
  • Step 4: Assemble the bowls
  • Step 5: Serve with garnishes
